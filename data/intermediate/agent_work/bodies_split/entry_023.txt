Comet Finlay.

by Kopff (Heidelberg) on 16 July; later, detected on a plate taken on 14 July. 
Located between Aquarius and Cetus, moving north-east; passed through 
Cetus, Taurus (August), Gemini (September-Ocotber) and Cancer.
