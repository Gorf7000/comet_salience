The second recorded apparition of the periodic Comet 
Brorsen-Metcalf (1847 V). Discovered by Metcalf (South Hero, U.S.A.). 
on 20 Aug. as a bright 8™ nebula in Pegasus. 
21 Aug., discovered independ-

1 Sept., Selivanov (Petrograd). 
First orbit calcula-

Moving very rapidly north, passed through Lacerta; 
31 Aug., located 
in Cepheus; at the beginning of September, traversed Cepheus, Draco and 
Ursa Minor, passing 13° from the pole; passed through the dipper of Ursa 
Major; 
in the second half of September, descended south; moved between 
Ursa Major and Canes Venatici; passed through Coma Berenices (beginning 
of October), Virgo, Hydra (October, November); located in Centaurus in 
December.

Holetschek (Vienna), Nijland (Utrecht), Hopmann (Boni), Jost(Johannesburg),
