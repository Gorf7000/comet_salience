1911 I (1910d). 
The fourth recorded apparition of the short-period 
Comet Brooks (2). Spotted according to Bauschinger's ephemeris by Aitken 
(Lick Obs.) on 28 Sept. 1910 as a very faint object (15 to 16™ apparently).

Discovery doubtful, yet comet observed by Barnard 
(Yerkes Obs.) on 29 Nov. with a 40-inch refractor in Capricornus; m=16™;
